## 🎁 **Tier Upgrade Benefits**

### **Why Upgrade from Free to Starter?**
- **ROI**: If you sell just ONE card for $30 on marketplace, the subscription pays for itself
- **Time Saved**: Price tracking saves hours of manual research
- **Professional**: Customers trust verified marketplace sellers

### **Why Upgrade from Starter to Professional?**
- **Commission Savings**: If you sell $5,000/month, 2% lower commission = $100/month saved
- **Automation**: Automated pricing saves 5+ hours/week
- **Better Rankings**: Priority listing = more sales

### **Why Upgrade from Professional to Enterprise?**
- **Scale**: Multiple stores = multiple revenue streams
- **Efficiency**: White-label + custom integrations = streamlined operations
- **Support**: Dedicated manager = faster issue resolution = less downtime
