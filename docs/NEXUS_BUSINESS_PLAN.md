## RISK ANALYSIS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Scanner Accuracy Below 95%
**Impact:** Shops won't adopt if manual verification still needed
**Mitigation:** 
- 100K+ card training dataset
- Continuous AI improvement
- Hybrid manual + AI verification mode
- Money-back guarantee during trial period

### Risk 2: Shops Resist New Technology
**Impact:** Slow adoption, low revenue
**Mitigation:**
- Free 90-day trial (no credit card)
- On-site setup and training
- Demonstrate immediate ROI (profit increase)
- Showcase testimonials from early adopters

### Risk 3: Consumer Scanner Manufacturing Delays
**Impact:** Miss launch window, lost revenue
**Mitigation:**
- Partner with proven manufacturer (Foxconn, Jabil)
- Build 6-month buffer into timeline
- Kickstarter pre-orders = validated demand
- Backup manufacturing partners lined up

### Risk 4: Patent Rejection or Violation
**Impact:** Lose competitive moat
**Mitigation:**
- File early (Q1 2026)
- Work with experienced patent attorney
- Provisional patent first, full patent later
- Trade secrets for some algorithms (don't patent everything)

### Risk 5: Competitor Emerges
**Impact:** Market share loss, price war
**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage (data moat)
- High switching costs (hardware investment)
- Network effects (more users = more value)
- Multiple revenue streams (can compete on price)

### Risk 6: Grading Services Not Accepted
**Impact:** $150M revenue stream at risk
**Mitigation:**
- Partner with existing graders first (PSA submission portal)
- Blockchain authentication (superior to PSA)
- Price 30-40% below PSA
- 5x faster turnaround
- AI pre-screening (higher consistency)
