### The Catch: Liquidation Preferences

Investors get paid first. Standard terms:

| Term | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| 1x liquidation pref | They get their money back before you get anything |
| Participating preferred | They get money back PLUS their % |
| Anti-dilution | If next round is down, they get more shares |

**Example:** Raised $10M total. Exit at $30M.
- Investors get $10M back first
- Remaining $20M split by ownership
- You (35%) get $7M, not $10.5M
- Investors get $17M total

At exits under $50M, you might get very little despite owning 35%.
