## What Has To Go Right

1. **First 50 shops love it** - This is everything
2. **Consumer scanner works** - Year 3 inflection
3. **Multi-category recognition is accurate** - The patent promise
4. **Network effects kick in** - More data = better AI
5. **No major competitor emerges** - Patent protection

## What Could Go Wrong

1. **Shops don't see value** - Need more beta testing
2. **Hardware reliability issues** - Quality control
3. **Recognition accuracy insufficient** - More training data
4. **Market downturn** - Collectibles are discretionary
5. **Patent challenged** - Prior art defense ready
