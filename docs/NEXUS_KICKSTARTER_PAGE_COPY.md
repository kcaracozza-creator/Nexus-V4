## SECTION 2: HOW IT WORKS

# How NEXUS Works

### Step 1: Drop the card in
Place any collectible card on the scanner bed. No orientation. No sorting. No prep.

### Step 2: AI does the rest
Dual 64MP cameras capture the card under cross-polarized lighting (zero glare, even on foils). Our AI identifies the card against a database of 106,000+ indexed cards across Magic: The Gathering, Pokémon, Yu-Gi-Oh, sports cards, and more.

### Step 3: Instant results
In under 2 seconds you get:
- Card name, set, and edition
- Real-time market price (multiple sources)
- Condition estimate (pre-grade prediction)
- Foil/variant detection
- A unique call number (like a library book)

### Step 4: Find it in seconds
Every card gets a call number. Customer asks for a card? Pull it in seconds, not hours. No more digging through shoeboxes.
