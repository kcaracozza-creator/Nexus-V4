# NEXUS Universal Collectibles
## 5-Year Blended Business Model
### Average of Conservative & Investor Projections

---

## Model Methodology

This model represents the **arithmetic mean** of two scenarios:
- **Conservative (Bootstrapped)**: Organic growth, minimal funding
- **Investor (Funded)**: Aggressive growth with venture capital

The blended model represents a **realistic middle ground** - achievable with moderate funding and focused execution.

## Why the Blended Model is Achievable

1. **Moderate funding** ($159M vs $317M investor model) reduces execution risk
2. **8.5% B2B market share** is aggressive but realistic with patent protection
3. **1.25% consumer penetration** requires strong word-of-mouth but achievable
4. **33% EBITDA margins** align with mature SaaS companies
5. **Hardware + Software moat** creates high switching costs

## Key Assumptions

- Patent protection holds (20 years from Nov 2025)
- No major competitor with similar technology emerges
- Consumer scanner ships by mid-2027
- AI recognition accuracy remains 95%+
- Collectibles market grows at historical 8% CAGR
